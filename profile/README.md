## 🧭 Who We Are

**UniX-AI-Lab** is an open, non-profit research collective advancing the **frontiers of artificial intelligence** across three intersecting pillars:

> **Visual Generation & Editing** · **Unified Modeling** · **Agentic Reasoning**

We believe the most impactful AI research happens in the open. Every model, dataset, codebase, and paper we produce is publicly released — no paywalls, no proprietary weights, no closed benchmarks.

Our work sits at the intersection of **academic rigor** and **practical capability**: we care deeply about *why* things work, not just that they do.

## 🔬 Research Pillars

<table>
<tr>
<td width="33%" valign="top">

### 🎨 Visual Gen & Editing
Pushing the frontier of image, video, and 3D generation — with precise, instruction-driven editing as a first-class citizen.

**Topics we explore:**
- Diffusion & flow-based generative models
- Instruction-following image/video editing
- Compositional scene generation
- 3D-consistent generation & NeRF/Gaussian integration
- Controllable synthesis (layout, style, identity)

</td>
<td width="33%" valign="top">

### 🧩 Unified Modeling
Breaking down the silos between modalities, tasks, and paradigms — building models that *understand and generate* across the full spectrum.

**Topics we explore:**
- Multimodal large language models (MLLMs)
- Any-to-any generation frameworks
- Unified architectures across vision, language, audio
- Tokenization & representation learning
- Cross-modal alignment & grounding

</td>
<td width="33%" valign="top">

### 🤖 Agentic Reasoning
Building AI systems that *plan, act, and reason* — not just respond. From tool-use and code generation to long-horizon scientific discovery.

**Topics we explore:**
- LLM-based autonomous agents
- Multi-agent collaboration & debate
- Tool-augmented reasoning (code, search, APIs)
- Scientific discovery automation
- Reinforcement learning for reasoning

</td>
</tr>
</table>

## 💡 Our Philosophy

```
Open Research  ≠  Slow Research
Non-Profit     ≠  Low Quality
Accessible     ≠  Trivial
```

We operate by a few core principles:

| Principle | What it means in practice |
|-----------|--------------------------|
| **Radical openness** | Code, weights, data, and evals — all public, always |
| **Depth over breadth** | We'd rather understand one thing well than five things superficially |
| **Community first** | Research should be reproducible, readable, and usable by everyone |
| **No gatekeeping** | No corporate sponsors dictating research direction |
| **Frontier, not incremental** | We swing for fundamental contributions, not 0.3% benchmark gains |

## 🤝 Join Us

UniX-AI-Lab is open to **researchers, engineers, and students** who share our mission.

**Ways to contribute:**
- 🐛 **Issues & PRs** — bug fixes, new features, documentation improvements
- 💬 **Research discussions** — open an issue to discuss ideas before implementing
- 📝 **Writing** — help us write papers, blog posts, or tutorials
- 🔁 **Reproduce** — replicate prior work and let us know what you find
- ⭐ **Star** — it helps other researchers find our work

**We especially welcome:**
- ML researchers at any career stage (PhD students very welcome!)
- Engineers who care about open-source infrastructure
- Anyone passionate about responsible, transparent AI development
